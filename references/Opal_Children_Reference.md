# Opal Children Reference

**Approved Image Style (Locked)**

This is the reference for the Opal Children in the story.

## Description
- Non-human children made entirely of living, shimmering opal/galaxy stone.
- Their **entire bodies** are polished, iridescent opal with internal nebula and star-like glows.
- Not normal human skin or clothing.
- Eyes like distant galaxies/nebulae.
- Innocent, child-like faces and expressions, but clearly otherworldly.
- Born from Note's grief and power after the explosions/loss.

## Key Scene Elements
- Small girl with radiant, joyful expression hugging Soraya’s leg tightly.
- Soraya in black niqab gently holding and kissing another glowing opal child on the forehead.
- Other opal children gathered around in a grand cosmic sanctuary.
- Warm, magical, peaceful family moment after great loss/grief.
- Note (First Flame) nearby watching with quiet love.
